Fig Toast Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This whipped goat cheese fig toast is made with cranberry walnut bread and topped with honey and chopped walnuts for a warm and sweet breakfast, lunch, snack, or even light dinner.

Preparation time:

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 min
  • Cooking:

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough
  • 3 tbsp ricotta cheese
  • 2 fresh figs
  • flake sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread: In the oven, toast your sourdough bread until it is slightly crispy and browned on edges, or to your desired doneness.
  2. Add ricotta: Spread about 1 1/2 tablespoons of fresh ricotta on each slice of toast.
  3. Add the figs: Place quartered figs on top of ricotta.
  4. Drizzle some honey: Drizzle toast with some of your favorite honey.
  5. Enjoy: Sprinkle with some sea salt and enjoy!
