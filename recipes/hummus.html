Healthy Hummus with Greek Yoghurt (No Oil)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/4 greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a blender or food processor. Blender gives better results.
  2. Process until smooth. Add more chickpea liquid/aquafaba for a smoother hummus. Adjust salt to taste.
  3. Transfer to plate and garnish with olive oil if desired.
