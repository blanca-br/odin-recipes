Fig Toast

Delicious Whipped Feta Cheese Fig Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough
  • 3 tbsp ricotta cheese
  • 2 fresh figs
  • flake sea salt

Steps:

  1. Toast your sourdough bread until it is slightly crispy and browned on edges, or to your desired doneness.
  2. Spread about 1 1/2 Tablespoons of fresh ricotta on each slice of toast.
  3. Place quartered figs on top of ricotta.
  4. Drizzle toast with some of your favorite honey.
  5. Sprinkle with salt.
